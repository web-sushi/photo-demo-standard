About Hana

Photographer based in Tokyo, Japan available for travel.

I specialize in capturing authentic moments with an editorial eye. Every session is a collaboration whether it's intimate portraits, couples in love, or meaningful events, I aim to tell your story with timeless elegance and genuine emotion.

7+ Years Experience Portraits • Couples • Events

Photography with intention

My journey into photography began with a simple fascination: how light, movement, and emotion come together in a single moment. What started as quiet observation slowly became a practice of storytelling.

Today, I focus on creating images that feel natural, refined, and honest. I guide when needed, step back when moments unfold naturally, and always aim for photographs that feel timeless rather than trendy.

Style & Approach

Light-filled, editorial-inspired imagery with a calm, documentary sensibility. Clean composition, subtle emotion, and natural tones.

What I Photograph

Portraits, couples, families, intimate weddings, small events, and select commercial projects.
